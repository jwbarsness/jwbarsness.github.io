BulletBorne

A procedural dungeon crawler where every bullet can damage both enemies and the player, creating a risk-reward combat system.

Role: Gameplay Programmer
Tech: C++
Focus: Combat Systems, Movement

My Contributions

  • Implemented player movement and collision handling
  • Handled gameplay interactions between player, enemies, and environment

Gameplay Systems

  • Projectile System
  • Risk-Reward Combat
  • Procedural Generation
  • Collision System

Challenges & Solutions

Collisions proved to be hazardous across the board, projectile collision also proved to be difficult as well. Precise calculations for bounces, interactions, collisions, and saving across rooms was the main iteration.
