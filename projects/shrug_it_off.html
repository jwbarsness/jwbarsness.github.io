Shrug It Off Port

Adds a chance for Armor Toughness, an often overlooked value, to block weak attacks.

Role: Lead Programmer
Tech: Java
Focus: Combat, Player Engagement

What does this Mod do?

  • Introduced a numerical calculation to use your armor toughness value to see if it can randomly stop a weak attack.
  • Configurable values to tweak the system.
  • Configurable blacklist to allow certain enemies or damage sources to always hit, like Fall Damage.

Gameplay Systems

  • Player Engagement
  • Randomness
  • Late Game Enjoyment
  • Feeling of Invincibility

Challenges & Solutions

The numerical system took careful implementation to follow and watch. Version mismatches of Minecraft led to a different implementation.
